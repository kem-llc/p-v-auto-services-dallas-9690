Our story

Your Trusted Local Auto Mechanic in Dallas

Our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep Dallas drivers safe and satisfied. We believe in transparency, integrity, and exceptional craftsmanship.

P V Auto Services was founded with a simple vision: create an auto repair shop where customers feel respected and valued. Our founder, a seasoned mechanic with over 15 years of experience, noticed that many shops prioritized profits over people. He set out to build a different kind of garage—one built on trust, fairness, and technical excellence.

Today, we continue that legacy. Our team of certified technicians serves the Dallas community with pride, treating every vehicle as if it were our own. From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we deliver dependable service at fair prices. We're not just fixing cars; we're building lasting relationships with our neighbors.
